Kassadin

Kassadin as a champion is pretty weak in the early game laning phase up until level 6. Up until level 6, remember to play extremely passively and try to last-hit minions with your Q. Biggest counters in this matchup are Talon, Zed, and Lucian. Try to make it to the end of laning phase without dying. Remember, you are a mid-late to a late game champion.

The Build

  1. Start of the game:
    • Dorans Ring
    • Health Potion (x2)
  2. First back:
    • Sheen
    • Tear of the Goddess
    • Lost Chapter
  3. Core to finish building first:
    • Lich Bane
    • Luden's Tempest
    • Archangel's Staff
  4. Late game core:
    • Rabbadon's Deathcap
    • Void Staff/Rylais/Zhonya's Hourglass
  5. Situational Items:
    • Warmogs
    • Guardian's Angel
    • Cosmic Drive

Kai'Sa

Kai'Sa is an extremely versatile champion as she can play as an ADC or as a solo-laner. I particularly like playing her as AP Kai'SA as sort of an sniper assassin. I believe that this buildpath forces her to play in a very unique type of style that not a lot of other champions in the game has. Similar playstyles would be champions like Lethality Varus, Xerath, and AP Nidalee (back in the day).
